PS5 Campaign

For a school project, I was tasked to create a campaign for the new PlayStation 5 set to release at the end of 2020. The campaign consists of three variations and was produced for multiple layouts and colour modes. Product images were taken from the official PlayStation Website. The photos were used for educational purposes only and have not/will not be reproduced in anyway. Done with Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign.
